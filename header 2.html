Header

Announcement Bar

From Admin >> Theme >> Top header and setting on the right. You can Turn ON or OFF the top announcement bar.

Top Bar

From Admin >> Theme >> Top header and setting on the right. You can Turn ON or OFF the top top bar.

Header Style

It includes the navigation bar and search, wishlist, cart icons. You can replace, turn on or off these sections in the right panel.

MegaMenu

From Navigation bar, you can create dropdown menu, megamenu include: Home, Shop, Blog, Page.
